FAQs

Q: Can I activate without an online account?
A: You can, but creating one makes management easier.

Q: Didn’t get a verification code?
A: Check email, SMS, or spam folder and request a new one.

Q: Can I activate by phone?
A: Yes, call the number on your card sticker and follow the prompts.
